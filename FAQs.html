Ordering & Products

Placing an order is simple! Just browse our website, add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, enter your delivery address, and choose your payment method. You'll receive a confirmation once the order is placed.

Delivery

We deliver from 7 AM to 10 PM every day. Delivery is free for all orders above ₹500. A small delivery fee of ₹40 is applied to orders below that amount.

Our lightning-fast delivery network aims to get your groceries to your doorstep often within minutes of placing an order. The exact time can vary based on your location and current order volume, but we always prioritize speed.

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via SMS and on the app. You can use this link to see the live location of your delivery executive.

Payments & Refunds

We accept all major payment methods, including Credit & Debit Cards, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, etc.), Net Banking, and major mobile wallets. We also offer Cash on Delivery (COD).

We have a "no questions asked" refund and return policy. If you're unhappy with the quality of any item, you can report it to our customer service within 24 hours of delivery for a full refund to your original payment source or wallet.
